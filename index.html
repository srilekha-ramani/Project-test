About US

Impact Match is a technology platform dedicated to fostering meaningful collaboration between students and non-profit organizations. We believe in the power of education combined with real-world experience to create positive social impact.

MISSION

To empower students to develop their skills and make a difference in their communities by connecting them with impactful volunteer opportunities at non-profit organizations.

VISION

To become the leading platform for non-profit and student collaboration, transforming the way volunteers connect, projects are executed, and positive change is achieved.
